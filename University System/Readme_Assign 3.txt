Employee and Student Classes have pure virtual functions salary and fee respectively. In child and grandchild classes of Employee, salary was fixed for each different
type of staff whichh is calculated by multiplying to their working hours and overtime.
SImilarly, Fee is fixed for each different category of student child classes calculated depending on the number of credit hours.
Faculty have one additional data member i.e. no of sections they teach which is also considered while calculating their salary.

Except that, all the arrays are dynamic size of which gets increased or decreased depending upon addition or deletion of record successfully.
